About ElevateX

ElevateX is a student-driven hackathon designed to encourage hands-on learning, peer-to-peer collaboration, and innovation. Built on the motto “Learn • Share • Uplift”, ElevateX provides a platform for students to transform ideas into impactful solutions within a limited time frame.

We encourage you to look around, find gaps, and build solutions.

Judging Criteria

  • Innovation and Originality
  • Problem Understanding
  • Solution Effectiveness
  • Technical Feasibility
  • Impact and Scalability
  • Presentation Quality

Rules & Guidelines

  • Teams must consist of 3 or 4 members.
  • Each participant can be part of only one team.
  • Participants must bring their own laptops and chargers.
  • Plagiarism or copied projects will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • All submissions must be completed before the deadline.
  • The judges’ decisions will be final and binding.
